Animaze 3D film process

All of the existing service-providers continue to participate under the Animaze process
Production
  • Initial 3D animation created by animators
  • Voices recorded in original language
  • Animaze Studio Stream tool used to apply original language
  • Animaze Lingo Stream tools learn entire movie and each character separately, to speed up entire process of production
  • Language distribution decisions are made
  • Local language scripts are written to exact same timing and approved by film studio
Post Production
  • The film (in low resolution) is sent out to voice recording studios, actors perform the voices
  • Voice recordings are sent to Animaze
  • Animaze Lingo Stream applies all voices to each character in each language version of the film
  • Quality control by the film studio of all language versions
Launch
  • Film forwarded by Animaze globally to distributors for theater release

Revolutionizing a growing market

The global animation and gaming market is expected to grow from $122.20 billion in 2010 to $242.93 billion by 2016. Now more than ever before, audiences around the world have an insatiable appetite for this engaging form of entertainment. High budget 3D animated productions are typically produced in the US and then manually dubbed through distributors for other markets in a manner that results in a suboptimal viewing experience for the audience.

Animaze technology automates the process of creating language versions of 3D animated productions! So creation of the original language version is automatic and precise, and for the first time, audiences outside of English-speaking countries also experience their language and the lips of the 3D animated characters in perfect synchronization.

Essentially, animated characters finally actually speak other languages. Animaze technology is a global 3D animation industry game changer that automates the current expensive and unsatisfactory process, while providing a seamless, high-quality viewing experience in any language.

The magic of our technology

Our software is unique and proprietary. Our process is the only one employing a learning engine-enabling threefold algorithm, using text-to-speech, speech-to-animation and character-behavior learning processes. Our internal and external output controllers allow animation to be exported under industry standard interfaces.



We offer three products:

  1. Lingo Stream (multi-language lip synch)
  2. Studio Stream (automatic sound analysis and lip synch)
  3. Geo Stream (change of scenery and written visuals according to territory)


Our unique technology and process gives studios greater control over their creative products and will potentially increase revenues of studios and distributors as animated films will no longer need to travel to many territories for dubbing, and consequently be pirated long before the official release date. Animaze technology can be applied to all formats and platforms used for distribution of 3D animated content including film, TV, video games, commercials, internet and mobile.
