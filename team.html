Management

Yossi Somer

CEO / Founder

Yossi is the driving force behind the establishment and development of ANIMAZE in Norway, serving the global animation industry. He is an award-winning director and producer with over thirty-five years of experience and an extensive international network in the motion picture industry. The Economist named him as one of the pioneers of cinematic “magical realism” because of his utilization of Computer Generated Images in motion picture production.

Dr. Nachshon Margaliot

CTO / COB / Co-Founder

Nachshon has been instrumental in establishing and developing numerous companies within the IT sector overfor the last twenty-five years. He was the founder of Speechview Ltd., a company that created the first animation-based phone for the hearing impaired. He has been involved in six start-ups over the last nine years and is currently co-founder/VP R&D of MAN Investments, which is focused on analyzing internet-based information to achieve better results in stock trading.

Evan Astrowsky

Hollywood CMO

Sharon Cohen

VP Dubbing & Studio Services

For the last twenty years Sharon has been working as a dubbing director, sound engineer, editor and actor in the dubbing field for major studios such as Pixar, Dreamworks and Warners. He has worked on films such as UP, Wall-E, Shrek the Third and How to Train Your Dragon. In this time he has learned the nature of lip sync and animation and is familiar with the challenges studios and technicians face, providing knowledge that enables ANIMAZE to better address the needs of major studios and can link the ANIMAZE solution to the world’s dubbing systems.

Kristian Aartun

PR & Promotion Manager

As the youngest Managing Director of a European record company, Edel Records AG Norway branch, Kristian started his career at the young age of 19. Under his leadership, 2.5 million CD's were sold in Norway before he moved on and became the Managing Director of EMI Publishing Norway AS. IN 2003 he founded his first radio station, Radio City, which became Norway's most popular radio station. In 2011 he started NIEC Norwegian International Entrepreneur Conference. Kristian is Executive Chairman and Founder of the Dutch Norwegian Business Network - DNBN.
